      The Guidebook

      Master these 6 principles to unlock the full potential of AI models.

      01

      Be Specific

      Detailed context prevents hallucinations. Instead of "Write about dogs," say "Write a 500-word blog post about the top 5 apartment dogs."

      02

      Assign a Role

      Persona adoption focuses the AI's knowledge base. "Act as a senior nutritionist with 10 years of experience" yields expert tone.

      03

      Define Format

      Never leave formatting to chance. Specify: "Provide output as a Markdown table," or "Return stringified JSON."

      04

      Give Examples

      Few-shot prompting shows the AI exactly what you want. Provide 1-2 input/output examples to set the pattern.

      05

      Set Constraints

      Define boundaries. "Under 200 words," "Use a 5th-grade reading level," or "Do not use robotic filler words."

      06

      Iterate

      The first prompt is rarely the best. Analyze the output, adjust your instructions, and try again. It's an iterative process.
